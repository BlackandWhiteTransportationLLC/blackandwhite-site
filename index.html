About Us

Black and White Transportation LLC will offer local and regional logistics solutions across the Southeastern U.S. using a compliance-based fleet structured for non-CDL operations. Our services are designed to support short- and mid-range transportation needs, including freight, delivery, and towing — all within DOT regulatory limits.

Why Choose Us

Built by professionals, for professionals. With a combined 40 years experience in the logistics, warehousing, and transportation industry, we’ve built Black and White Transportation because we understand the grind — because we've lived it. We’re committed to building a company that respects hard work, understands and values fair pay, and delivers top-tier service from the inside out.

Services

Local and regional transport solutions including box trucks and dually/gooseneck trailer setups, while serving DOT compliance for non-CDL operations providing diverse logistics needs.
