About Us

Black and White Transportation LLC is a minority-owned logistics company based in Gulf Coast Alabama, dedicated to delivering non-CDL compliant freight and towing solutions throughout the Southeastern U.S. We are driven by purpose and built for the long haul — creating a company founded on experience, trust, and community values. We believe in empowering workers, honoring hard work, and setting a new standard in local and regional transportation services.

Services

We will offer local and regional logistics solutions across the Southeastern U.S. using a compliance-based fleet structured for non-CDL operations. Our services are designed to support short- and mid-range transport needs, including freight, delivery, and towing — all within DOT regulatory limits.
